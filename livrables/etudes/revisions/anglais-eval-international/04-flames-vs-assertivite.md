# 04 — Flames vs Assertivité polie

## Qu'est-ce qu'un flame ?

Un **flame** est un e-mail professionnel agressif, insultant ou hostile. La compétence évaluée : **transformer un message agressif en message poli mais assertif** (= ferme mais courtois).

## Termes agressifs à reconnaître

| Expression agressive | Sens | Niveau |
|---|---|---|
| An utter disgrace | Une honte absolue | Très agressif |
| To foul things up | Tout gâcher, faire une grosse boulette | Agressif |
| Pathetic | Pathétique, lamentable | Insultant |
| To sue / To see someone in court | Poursuivre en justice | Menace juridique |
| Probationary period | Période d'essai (souvent utilisé comme menace) | Menace implicite |

## Tableau des réécritures (À MAÎTRISER)

| ❌ Agressif / direct | ✅ Formel et poli |
|---|---|
| I can't reach you by phone | **Unfortunately, I am unable to reach you by phone** |
| Are you making progress? | **Can you tell me how you are managing to make progress?** |
| You haven't sent anything | **Our records show we have not received anything** |
| You debited my account | **You have proceeded with debiting our account** |
| I want an apology | **We should like a formal apology** |
| To make oneself clear | **To ensure I have made myself clear** |

## Les techniques de transformation

### 1. Passer du "you" accusateur au "we" ou au passif
- *You haven't sent anything* → *Our records show we have not received anything*
- *You debited my account* → *You have proceeded with debiting our account*

### 2. Utiliser des modaux de politesse
- *I want* → *We should like* / *I would like*
- *Can you...* → *Could you...* / *Would you be able to...*

### 3. Ajouter des amortisseurs (softeners)
- *Unfortunately, ...*
- *I'm afraid that ...*
- *It would appear that ...*
- *With all due respect, ...*

### 4. Passer d'une affirmation à une question
- *You're wrong* → *Could there be a misunderstanding?*
- *Fix this now* → *Could this be resolved promptly?*

### 5. Reformuler les menaces en demandes formelles
- *I'll sue you* → *We may have to consider legal action if no resolution is found*
- *I want an apology* → *We should like a formal apology*

## Mini glossaire de l'assertivité

| Mot / expression | Usage |
|---|---|
| Unfortunately | Pour introduire un fait négatif poliment |
| Our records show | Pour évoquer un fait sans accuser |
| We should like / We would like | Demande polie |
| To ensure | Pour confirmer sans agresser |
| To proceed with | Action neutre, professionnelle |
| At your earliest convenience | Dès que possible (poli) |
| We appreciate | Remerciement formel |
| With reference to | En référence à |

## Exemple complet de transformation

### Version flame ❌
> Your service is an **utter disgrace**. You've **fouled things up** completely and **I want an apology** now or **I'll see you in court**. This is **pathetic**.

### Version assertive et polie ✅
> *I am writing to express my concern regarding the quality of service received. Unfortunately, several issues have arisen which require immediate attention. We should like a formal apology, and we trust this matter can be resolved without resorting to further action. Please confirm your proposed resolution at your earliest convenience.*
