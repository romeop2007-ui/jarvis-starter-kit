## Exercice 2 — Choisis le bon idiome

1. *I really ____ when I asked my new colleague about her divorce.* (put my foot in it / broke the ice / saw eye to eye)
2. *We ____ with our Dutch partners — we agree on everything.* (get on like a house on fire / are thrown in at the deep end / put our foot in it)
3. *On my first day, nobody trained me. I was completely ____.* (a real eye-opener / thrown in at the deep end / fed up)
4. *She told a funny story to ____ at the start of the meeting.* (break the ice / get into hot water / ring off)
5. *Visiting Japan was ____ — I learned so much about their culture.* (a fish out of water / a real eye-opener / a chatterbox)
