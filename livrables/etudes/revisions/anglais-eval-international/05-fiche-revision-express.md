## ☎️ CHATTERBOX — Vocabulaire clé
- **Chatterbox** = bavard(e)
- **Get down to business** = passer aux choses sérieuses
- **Cut the chat** = couper court
- **Ring off** = raccrocher
- **Abrupt** = brusque
- **Drastic measures** = mesures radicales

## 📞 COMPLAINT HANDLING — Les 6 étapes (G-L-G-S-A-E)
1. **G**reet and reassure
2. **L**isten and empathise
3. **G**et the details
4. **S**uggest solutions
5. **A**gree on a course of action
6. **E**nd on a positive note

## 🔥 FLAMES → ASSERTIVITÉ
- *I can't reach you* → **Unfortunately, I am unable to reach you**
- *You haven't sent* → **Our records show we have not received**
- *I want an apology* → **We should like a formal apology**
- *You debited my account* → **You have proceeded with debiting our account**

## ⚠️ PIÈGES À ÉVITER
- **To ring off** (raccrocher) pas "to hang up"
- **Chatterbox** = personne (pas chatbot)
- L'ordre des 6 étapes de complaint : **Greet AVANT Listen**
- **Probationary period** = période d'essai (peut être une menace dans un flame)
