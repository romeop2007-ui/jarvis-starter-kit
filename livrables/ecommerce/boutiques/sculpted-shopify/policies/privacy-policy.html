Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sculpted ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects your personal data when you visit or make a purchase from our store. Sculpted is a trading name of Roméo PIAT, a sole trader registered in France (SIRET 10549697000010). We act as the data controller for the personal data described below. We are committed to handling your data in line with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

1. Information we collect

When you browse our store, place an order or contact us, we may collect:

  • Order and contact details: name, billing and shipping address, email address, phone number.
  • Payment information: processed securely by our payment providers. We do not store your full card details.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information and how you interact with our store, collected through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How we use your information

  • To process and deliver your orders, including arranging shipping with our fulfilment partners.
  • To communicate with you about your order, respond to enquiries and provide customer support.
  • To send marketing emails where you have given consent (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • To prevent fraud, improve our store and meet our legal obligations.

3. Sharing your information

To fulfil your order we share the data necessary for delivery with trusted third parties, including:

  • Our e-commerce platform (Shopify), which hosts our online store.
  • Payment processors, to take payment securely.
  • Our suppliers and shipping/fulfilment partners, who require your name and delivery address to ship your order.

We never sell your personal data. Some partners may be located outside the UK; where that is the case we take appropriate steps to ensure your data is protected.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to operate the store, remember your basket, analyse traffic and (with your consent) measure advertising. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data retention

We keep your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described above, including to meet accounting and legal requirements.

6. Your rights

Under the UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict the processing of your personal data, to object to processing, and to data portability. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sculpted.communication@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

7. Contact

For any question about this Privacy Policy or your data, email us at sculpted.communication@gmail.com.
